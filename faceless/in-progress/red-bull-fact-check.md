## 2. Dietrich Mateschitz Biographical Facts

### Birth Date and Location
**Claim:** Born May 20, 1944 in Sankt Marein im Murztal, Styria, Austria

**Verdict:** ✅ VERIFIED

**Source:** [Wikipedia - Dietrich Mateschitz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dietrich_Mateschitz) confirms: "Dietrich Markwart Eberhart Mateschitz (20 May 1944 - 22 October 2022) was an Austrian entrepreneur" born in Sankt Marein im Murztal, Styria.

---

### University Duration
**Claim:** Took 10 years to complete marketing degree at Vienna University

**Verdict:** ✅ VERIFIED

**Source:** Multiple sources including [Formula 1](https://www.formula1.com/en/latest/article/obituary-remembering-dietrich-mateschitz-red-bull-and-alphatauris-quiet.7CNRiGEhYVanRPbQAx79y) and biographical articles confirm he took approximately 10 years to complete his marketing degree at the Vienna University of Economics and Business, working as a ski instructor and doing labor jobs between classes.

---

### Blendax Position
**Claim:** Worked at Blendax as Director of International Marketing for toothpaste

**Verdict:** ✅ VERIFIED

**Source:** [Wikipedia - Dietrich Mateschitz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dietrich_Mateschitz), [Encyclopedia.com](https://www.encyclopedia.com/journals/culture-magazines/mateschitz-dietrich), and [BM Magazine](https://bmmagazine.co.uk/entrepreneur-interviews/dietrich-mateschitz-one-of-the-worlds-leading-salesman-dies-aged-78/) all confirm he was "director of international marketing" at Blendax working on toothpaste marketing.

---

### TC Pharmaceutical Connection
**Claim:** TC Pharmaceutical was Blendax's Thai distributor

**Verdict:** ✅ VERIFIED

**Source:** [Black Tomato](https://www.blacktomato.com/us/inspirations/the-business-of-traveldietrich-mateschitz/) and other sources confirm: "A marketing executive for German consumer goods firm Blendax, he'd just arrived in Bangkok... to visit the company's local distributor, which happened to be Yoovidhya's TC Pharmaceutical Industries."

---

### Discovery Year
**Claim:** Discovered Krating Daeng in 1982

**Verdict:** ✅ VERIFIED

**Source:** [Wikipedia - Krating Daeng](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Krating_Daeng) and multiple sources confirm: "In 1982, Austrian marketing executive Dietrich Mateschitz was visiting Thailand and discovered that Krating Daeng effectively cured his jet lag."

---

### Net Worth at Death
**Claim:** Net worth $27.4 billion at death in 2022

**Verdict:** ✅ VERIFIED

**Source:** Multiple financial sources confirm Mateschitz was worth approximately $27.4 billion when he died on October 22, 2022.

---

### Ranking in Austria
**Claim:** Richest person in Austria at death

**Verdict:** ✅ VERIFIED

**Source:** Multiple sources confirm Dietrich Mateschitz was the wealthiest person in Austria at his death.
